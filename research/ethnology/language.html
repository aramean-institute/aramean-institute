Aramaic is a language or group of languages that belong to the Semitic language family. The language has gone through 5 development phases according to the analysis done by Joseph Augustine Fitzmyer and Aaron Butts: Old Aramaic, Official Aramaic, Middle Aramaic, Late Aramaic, and Modern Aramaic.
