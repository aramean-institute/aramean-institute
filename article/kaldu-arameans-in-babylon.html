  2. The Kaldu-Arameans in Babylon

Chaldeans are Arameans from the tribe of Kaldu or (Kasdu) conquering and destroying the Assyrian Empire.

The Chaldeans under ruler Nabopolassar of Neo-Babylon formed a powerful alliance (c. 620 BC) with the Persians, Medes, Scythians, Cimmerians, Israelites, and other tribes to overthrow the harsh Assyrian rulers and their long-lasting empire.


In 612 BC, the Assyrian city of Nineveh was besieged and sacked by the alliance after a brutal battle. The fall of Nineveh led to the destruction of the Neo-Assyrian Empire and the Assyrian people over the few years.
