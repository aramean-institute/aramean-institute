  2. The holy language of Tur Abdin

The Aramaic of Tur Abdin (Turabdium) is the Aramaic of Urhoy (Edessa). "Aramaya" became "Suryaya" and "Suryaya" became "Suryoyo".


Aramaya became the most important dialect of Late Aramaic.
