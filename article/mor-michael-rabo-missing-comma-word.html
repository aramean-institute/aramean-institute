Misstakes made in The Chronicles of Michael Rabo interpretation

  1. Article
  2. Misstakes made in The Chronicles of Michael Rabo interpretation

When Theologian Jean Baptist Chabot released the first edition of his interpretation of The Chronicles of Michael Rabo, he failed to add a comma and the word "Oromoye" to the sentence.


The error result went viral online for years and confused many Arameans when it claimed "Othuroye D-henun Suryoye", meaning "Assyrians are Syriacs".


In the first edition, it is clear that it says "Shmohe d-'ame d-ith l-hun sefro d-men bnayo d-Shim", in the subheading but nothing about "Oromoye" in the body text. When reading further on page 11, it reads: "Bnay Shim: Othuroye, Kaldoye, Ludoye, Oromoye D-henun Suryoye, Ebroye, Forsoye"
