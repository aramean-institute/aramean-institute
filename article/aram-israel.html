The Bible reveals that Arameans and the Hebrew Patriarchs have shared origins and marriage ties existed between the peoples.


Both Arameans and Hebrews (Israelites) are a Semitic race tracing their lineage back to Shem, son of Noah, but what ties their bound even stronger is Aram, son of Shem.


Aram, the son of Shem, played a role in forming many nations and spreading his offsprings in the Middle East. He is regarded as being the ancestor of the Arameans and the Jewish people.
