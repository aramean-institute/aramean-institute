  2. Israel recognizes the Arameans as separate nationality

A significant and historical event: Israel is the first country in the world to acknowledge Aramean minoritiy living in Israel as a distinct ethnicity.

Several scholars consulted with the Ministry of Interior before making the announcement, and based on their advice Minister Gideon Sa'ar ruled that "the presence of the Aramean nation is evident."


The historical heritage, religion, culture, origin and shared language met the conditions of proving the existince of the Aramean nation.


In December 2014, the Registration and Passports Section of the Population and Immigration Authority published its guidelines on the subject, whose provision permits Arameans to claim their identities.
