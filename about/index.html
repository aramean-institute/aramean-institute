About Us

We are a worldwide multi-institute, researching different areas, developing technology, collecting data, publishing work, offering expertise, and educating about the Aramean people.

Mission

Our mission is to connect and build long-term relationships with companies, organizations, and individuals from all nations. We aim to provide reliable and trusted knowledge and data, share our expertise about the Aramean people, and offer valuable services. While some of our offerings are complimentary, our primary goal is to foster understanding and collaboration.


The Aramean Institute's standard work approaches are:

  • Correcting misleading, unlogic, or false knowledge about the Arameans.
  • Providing reliable-trusted information and data about the Arameans.
  • Developing software related to the Arameans.
  • Increasing the awareness of Arameans to the outside world.
  • Guiding and supporting projects related to the Arameans.
  • Translating Aramaic to and from any other world language.

Vision

Our vision is to preserve, protect, and promote the timeless Aramean heritage, fostering unity among Native Syrian (Syriac) people through the power of education and scientific knowledge under the Aramean umbrella.

History

The Aramean Institute was established in late 2020 by Josef Gabrielsson in Sweden.

Principles

The Aramean Institute follows the principles of Science and has built the foundation on academic studies made by reliable, respected, and world-leading scholars.

The institute rejects:

  • Theories outside the academic fields.
  • Statements or claims based on fanatism or rationalism.
  • Propaganda or political agendas.

Contribution

The Aramean Institute thrives on the collective contributions of its community.

Information

We collect and publish valuable insights and knowledge contributed by experienced Syriacists and Arameanists.

Software code

Our institute is built on an Open Source foundation, where individuals from all walks of life can contribute code to enhance our software development capabilities.

Financing

As a nonprofit organization, we ensure that every contribution goes directly into sustaining our mission. We rely on the generous financial support of individuals and organizations, whose contributions keep the Aramean Institute running and thriving.
