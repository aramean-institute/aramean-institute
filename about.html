About Us

The Aramean Institute is a multi-institute for the Aramean people researching different areas, developing technology, collecting data, educating, offering expertise, and publishing work.

Mission

Our mission is to connect and build long-term relationships with companies, organizations, and private people from all nations, provide reliable-trusted knowledge and data, offer free services, and share our expertise about or related to the Aramean people.


The Aramean Institute's standard work approaches are:

  • Correcting misleading, unlogic, or false knowledge about the Arameans.
  • Providing free, reliable-trusted information and data about the Arameans.
  • Developing free software related to the Arameans.
  • Increasing the awareness of Arameans to the outside world.
  • Guiding and supporting projects related to the Arameans.
  • Translating Aramaic to

Vision

Our vision is to protect, populate, and preserve the continuous ancient Aramean heritage.

History

The instutute was established 2020 by Josef Gabrielsson in Sweden.

Principles

The Aramean Institute follows the principles of Science and has built the foundation on academic studies made by reliable, respected, and world-leading scholars.

The institute rejects:

  • Theories outside the academic fields.
  • Statements and claims based on fanatism or rationalism.
  • Propaganda or political agendas.

Financing

The Institute is financied by Aramean organizations located in different parts of the world.
