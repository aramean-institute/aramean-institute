About us

Mission

Our mission is to increase the awareness of Arameans to the outside world and provide reliable-trusted knowledge about the Aramean people.

It includes correcting Non-Academic studies or tackling misleading knowledge about the Arameans or related to the people so that we may preserve and protect the ancient heritage and language.


We work with culture, technology, education, science, and business to strengthen relationships and development within the private, public, and voluntary sectors.

History

The instutute was established 2020 by Josef Gabrielsson in Sweden.

Principles

The Aramean Institute of Arameans follows the principles of Science and has built the foundation on academic studies made by reliable, respected, and world-leading scholars.

The institute rejects:

  • Theories outside the academic fields.
  • Statements and claims based on fanatism or rationalism.
  • Propaganda or political agendas.

Financing

The Institute is financied by Aramean organizations located in different parts of the world.
