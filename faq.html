The Aramean homeland (Aram & Aram Naharaim) was conquered by non indegiounus people such as Arabs, Ottomans, Assyrians, Kurds, Persians, Greeks etc., however Syria (Aram) is considered the modern homeland of the Arameans, but under Arab rule.

  
        
    

Due to the Colonization of the Middle East and, persecutions of Arameans, groups of the Arameans pledge alliances with the Westerners, in hope of being independent one day, and to restore the Middle East to its former glory. The ancient names such as Assyrians and Chaldeans were chosen accordingly to the geographical location of Assyria and Babylon provinces. The Syriac Churches of the East and the national separatist movements propagated the new identities amongst the people and created a bogus ethnology.
